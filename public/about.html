About Me

Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites, encompassing various tasks and disciplines to ensure a seamless online experience.
It involves front-end development, which focuses on the visual and interactive aspects of a website using technologies like
HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and back-end development, which handles server-side logic, databases, and application integration using frameworks and languages such as Python, PHP, or Node.js.
Modern web development also emphasizes responsive design, ensuring sites function well across devices, and incorporates practices like SEO optimization, security enhancements, and
performance tuning. The field continues to evolve rapidly, driven by advancements in tools, frameworks, and technologies, enabling developers to craft dynamic, scalable, and user-friendly web applications that meet diverse business and user needs.
